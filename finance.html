    A word of advice

    There will be times that an investor such as yourself will become emotionally attached to investing. Essentially, you'll have the feeling to constantly check on your portofilio and exagerate any losses. Here's a couple of tips.

    1. Never invest any amount of money that you will need.
    2. Always have money on the side and in a savings account.
    3. Do your research, never rely on a amatuer to do your investing.
    4. The news is your friend, it keeps you in the know within the markets.
    5. Buy a stock at its lowest price, sell at its highest price.
    6. Sometimes just breath before making any decisions to buy or sell a stock.
    7. Never listen to ads about buying a specific stock, most of them are promotations.
    8. Be careful of stocks that suddenly rise in price, they can quickly go down as well.
    9. Use your time wisely, its important to read more books or articles about the stock market and its functions.
